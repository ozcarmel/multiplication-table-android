# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

## Overview

Multiplication Made Easy / לוח הכפל בקלות is an educational multiplication table app for children.

The app is designed to work without collecting personal information.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information.

The app does not ask for:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Photos
- Contacts
- Account login
- Payment information

## Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for children and families.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Internet and Servers

The app does not require a user account and does not send answers or practice activity to a server.

## Advertising

The app does not contain ads.

## Analytics

The app does not include analytics SDKs.

## In-App Purchases

The app does not include in-app purchases.

## Data Sharing

Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not share personal information with third parties.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated if the app changes in the future. Any future version that collects data should update this policy before release.
